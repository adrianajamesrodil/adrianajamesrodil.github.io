Poverty in the Island

According to the U.S. Census Bureau, while there there was a 1.3% decrease in poverty in Puerto Rico from 2017 to 2018, the island still faces higher poverty than the U.S. national rate of 13.1%. Moreover, Mississippi – the state with the highest poverty rate, with 19.7% – has less than double the poverty rate of Puerto Rico. Given the facts aforementioned above, however, this comes as no surpise as a result of the statistics related to computer and internet access and education levels.
